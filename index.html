About Us

Zivira Labs: For eyes that envision a better tomorrow!

At Zivira Labs, we are driven by a singular purpose to protect and preserve the gift of sight. Since our inception in 2017 in India, we’ve grown with a bold global vision, rapidly becoming one of the fastest growing names in ophthalmic pharmaceuticals.

Our diverse and evolving portfolio is designed to protect and enhance wide spectrum of eye care. From everyday concerns to complex, long-term conditions, we deliver trusted solutions backed by science, quality, commitment and care.

At Zivira, we don’t just develop products for the eyes, we create possibilities for brighter futures. Because better vision empowers better lives!

Vision

Dedicated to advancing eye health through pioneering research, state-of-the-art manufacturing practices, and a steadfast commitment to patient-centric innovation.

Mission

Committed to deliver safe, effective, and accessible medications that exceed the expectations of healthcare professionals and patients alike.

Values

Quality | Patient centricity | Innovation | Integrity | Responsibility
