Tizta

TIZTA ® is a lubricant eye drops used to relieve burning, irritation, and discomfort due to dryness of the eye.

NOTE: You should discuss the potential risks and benefits of all the eye drops with your doctor. Zivira products are prescription-based and are used to treat symptoms of dry eye. Consult your doctor for advice on using TIZTA ® . Your doctor will decide the dosage of TIZTA ® depending on your age, body weight, and severity of your condition.

  • Composition :
    Sodium Hyaluronate BP 0.1%
  • Presentation :
    Tizta (10 ml)
  • Indication / Uses :
    Used as a lubricant for
    • Temporary relief from burning, irritation, and discomfort in the eye and
    • Relief from discomfort due to environmental elements such as wind, smoke, sun, dry air, air conditioning & prolonged use of computers
  • Dosage / Application :
    One drop at a time to the eye is given 5 – 6 times daily or as directed by the physician
  • Safety Information :
    Tizta eye drops rarely cause serious side effects or an allergic reaction. For a few moments, vision may temporarily get blurred after the instillation of the eye drops. Minor irritation and burning may occur temporarily.
  • Drug interactions :
    There are no known drug interactions. In co-administration with other eye medication, it should always be applied last, after at least 5 minutes.
  • Overdose :
    There are no known reports of overdosing.
  • Storage & Handling:
    Store below 30ºC. Protect from light & moisture. Do not freeze.
  • Warnings and precautions :
    • Keep out of reach of children.
    • NOT FOR INJECTION. FOR EXTERNAL USE ONLY.
    • Use the solution within one month after opening the container.

NOTE: Call your doctor or get medical care immediately if you develop any side effects.

The associated risks and benefits of using TIZTA ® in pregnant women and breastfeeding mothers are unclear. However, no preventive measures are advised for using TIZTA ® products in pregnant women. Consult your doctor for advice.
