Tizta Liquigel

TIZTA™ liquigel is an eye lubricant that may help heal the damage to the eye’s surface resulting from a lack of lubrication. The long-lasting film protects the surface of the eye during the healing process and moistens, soothes, and lubricates the surface of the eye.

NOTE: You should discuss the potential risks and benefits of all the eye drops with your doctor. Zivira products are prescription-based and are used to treat symptoms of dry eye. Consult your doctor for advice on using TIZTA™ liquigel. Your doctor will decide the dosage of TIZTA™ liquigel depending on your age, body weight, and severity of your condition.

  • Composition :
    Sodium hyaluronate BP
  • Presentation :
    Tizta Liquigel (10 ml)
  • Indication / Uses :
    TIZTA™ liquigel acts as artificial tears and helps relieve burning sensation, dryness, and discomfort. It provides stable, long-lasting lubrication of the eye surface.
  • Dosage / Application :
    As directed by the physician.
  • Safety Information :
    drug interaction, overdose
  • Storage :
    Store at a temperature not exceeding 30°C. Protect from light and moisture. Do not freeze.
Use of this product may occasionally cause intolerance (mild burning or irritation).
  • Keep out of reach children.
  • Do not use TIZTA™ eye drops if allergic to any of its contents.
  • Avoid touching the tip of the dropper as it may contaminate the product.
  • TIZTA™ drops might blur the vision immediately after use; therefore, avoid driving or operating machinery until your vision is clear.
  • Not for injection. For ophthalmic use only.

NOTE: Call your doctor or get medical care immediately if you develop any side effects.

The associated risks and benefits of using TIZTA™ liquigel in pregnant women and breastfeeding mothers are unclear. However, no preventive measures are advised for using TIZTA™ liquigel products in pregnant women. Consult your doctor for advice.
